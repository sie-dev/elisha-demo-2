🚀 How This Works

This interface searches through the Chabad corpus by:

  1. Loading JSON files from the Dropbox dataset
  2. Searching for your term across all texts
  3. Extracting relevant passages with full context
  4. Using Claude AI to analyze and explain the findings
  5. Providing explanations in Hebrew, Yiddish, and English
